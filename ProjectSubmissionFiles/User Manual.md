# User Manual - Student Tracker App

### User Accounts & Login
#### Creating an account  
> To Create an account simply navigate to the login page(should automatically be here if you are not logged in yet). Once on this page simply click the create account hyperlink beneath the login button. This will bring you to the create account page where you will input all account information and submit it.
#### Logging In
> To Log into the student tracker app, first make sure you have an account, then input your credentials to the Log in page and press *Enter* or click the login button.
#### Logging Out
> simply click *Logout* on the top right of the nav bar.
---
### Messages
#### View and Send messages
> To view and send messages simply click *messages* on the nav bar. This will bring you to the contacts page where you can see your contacts and their most recent message as well as if you have read it or not.
> To send a message simply click one of the contacts and input your message in the text field at the bottom of the screen, then press *Enter* or click *send*

#### Send Group Notification(Teachers)
> Navigate to the dashboard page using the nav bar or just by logging in. Once there scroll to the bottom of the page where you'll see a text input. Simply input your desired group notification to all students and hit send.
---
### Dashboard
#### Check-In(Students)
> To check in simply click the check in button on the student dashboard once you are ready to check in. When you are ready to check out input your session notes and simply click check out.
#### View Check-In's(Teachers)
> Simply open your dashboard and you should see each student listed on your dashboard. Clicking on one of these students will reveal their check-in's. Clicking to expand one of these check-in's will display the check-in notes.
